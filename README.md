# Preventing Aging — Research Repository

A living research document tracking the state of longevity science with a focus on **preventing and reversing aging mechanisms** — not the science fiction of immortality, but the real molecular biology of why we age and what interventions show genuine evidence.

## Philosophy

The goal is not "immortality" in a fantasy sense. It's understanding and intervening in the specific biological mechanisms that cause aging:

- Why do cells accumulate damage?
- Why does epigenetic regulation drift over time?
- Why do stem cells exhaust?
- What can we actually do about it today, and what's on the horizon?

This repo holds rigorously sourced research, trimmed of hype, focused on evidence quality.

## Evidence Quality Standard

Entries are labeled with confidence level:
- **High confidence** — replicated human RCT data
- **Moderate confidence** — strong animal data + early human trials
- **Low/Speculative** — interesting preclinical data only

Items in the "speculative" category are clearly flagged and candidates for trimming as the evidence evolves.
